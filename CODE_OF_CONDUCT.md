# Code of Conduct

SkillGate contributors are expected to keep technical collaboration respectful, evidence-based, and safe.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss code, data, and research claims without personal attacks.
- Give actionable feedback and distinguish verified findings from speculation.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licensing, benchmark blinding, and responsible disclosure.
- Welcome contributors regardless of background, identity, experience, or affiliation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate exposure of private information, credential sharing, plagiarism, benchmark leakage, or retaliation are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the hosting platform. Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or escalate serious threats. Reports should be handled confidentially, consistently, and without retaliation.

This policy applies to repositories, reviews, discussions, issue trackers, study coordination, and project events.
